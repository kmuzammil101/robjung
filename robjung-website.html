// The Author

Lawyer. Dreamer.
Storyteller.

Rob Jung has been a lawyer, a sawyer, inventor and singer; an entrepreneur, provocateur, historian and thespian. He's cooked gourmet meals, driven race cars, and guided fishing trips in places most people never find on a map.

Born in California's wine country, raised on a Wisconsin riverbank, and trained at Harvard Law. He now lives the writer's life in suburban St. Paul, Minnesota — producing thrillers sharp enough to draw blood and publishing other authors through Hawk Hill Literary, an Ingram imprint.
